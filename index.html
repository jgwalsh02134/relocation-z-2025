Professional photos + staging; strategic list at $999,000; launch marketing; open houses.

Tour thoroughly; request HOA resale packet; submit competitive offer with focused contingencies.

Inspections (general + radon); appraisal; mortgage commitment; HOA review; insurance quotes.

Week 6–8: Closings

Close on White Plains; final walkthrough; close on Loudonville; utilities transfer; change of address.
